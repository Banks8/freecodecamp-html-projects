Fried Hake Recipe

This is a succulent recipe of the famous fried hake

Ingredients

  • Hake filet
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Black/white Pepper
  • Sunflower Oil
  • Milk

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs and milk into a bowl
  2. In a separate bowl, mix flour, salt and pepper
  3. Heat a pan and pour the sunflower oil to fill the bottom surface
  4. Dip the hake filet into the egg and milk bowl
  5. Remove the hake and put it in the flour bowl to completely cover with flour
  6. Move the hake filet to the pan with hot oil so that it is fried until golden brown
  7. serve with any side of your choice
